FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How often are metal rates updated?+
Rates are refreshed automatically once every day via a scheduled data pipeline, and shown with an exact last-updated timestamp on every page.
Are these rates the same as MCX or LME prices?+
Rates are derived from international benchmark prices converted to INR per kilogram, meant as a reliable reference for scrap and trading decisions rather than an exact exchange tick.
Can I see historical prices for a metal?+
Yes — every metal has its own page with 7-day, 30-day and 1-year historical charts, plus 52-week high, low and average.
Do you provide rates for different scrap grades?+
Each metal page includes a scrap grade table showing typical price ranges for the grades dealers commonly buy and sell.
