Copper rate — frequently asked questions

What is today's Copper rate per kg in India?+
Today's copper rate is shown live at the top of this page in INR per kilogram, updated daily.
Why does the Copper rate change daily?+
Copper prices track international commodity benchmarks and the USD-INR exchange rate, both of which move daily based on demand, supply and currency markets.
What is the difference between copper scrap grades?+
Scrap grades differ by purity and contamination level, which changes the price a dealer will pay — see the grade table above for typical ranges.
Is this the official MCX or LME copper rate?+
This is a reference rate derived from benchmark prices converted to INR per kg, intended to guide scrap and trade decisions rather than serve as an exact exchange quote.
