Privacy Policy

Metal Rates India does not require account creation to view rates. We do not collect personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary to operate the website.

Local storage: We use your browser's local storage only to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode) and, for the optional Admin panel, a session flag. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

WhatsApp opt-in: If you join our WhatsApp broadcast list, your number is handled directly by WhatsApp under their privacy policy; we do not store numbers on this website.

Analytics: If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated to disclose the provider and data collected.

For any privacy questions, please reach out via the Contact page.
