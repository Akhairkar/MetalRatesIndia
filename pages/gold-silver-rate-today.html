What is the difference between 24K and 22K gold?+
24K gold is 99.9% pure with no alloying, while 22K (91.6% pure) is mixed with metals like copper or silver for added strength, commonly used in jewellery.
What is the difference between 999 and 925 silver?+
999 silver is fine silver at 99.9% purity, typically used for coins and bars, while 925 (sterling) silver is 92.5% pure, alloyed with other metals for jewellery durability.
Do gold rates include GST and making charges?+
No — the rates shown are base metal prices only. Jewellers add GST and making charges separately at the time of purchase.
